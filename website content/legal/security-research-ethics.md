# Security Research Ethics

_Last updated: (insert date)_

## Authorized Testing Only
All demonstrations and experiments documented on this site are performed in lab environments that I own or am authorized to use.

I do not conduct unauthorized access, scanning, or testing against third‑party systems.

## Responsible Conduct
My work follows these principles:
- Respect for privacy
- No exploitation of real users
- No testing against production systems without permission
- Compliance with applicable laws and policies

## Disclosure
If I discover a security issue in a third‑party product or service, I follow responsible disclosure practices and report it privately to the vendor where appropriate.
