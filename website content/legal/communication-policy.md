# Communication Policy

_Last updated: (insert date)_

## Purpose
The contact options on this website are provided for professional inquiries and networking related to employment or collaboration opportunities.

## Appropriate Messages
Appropriate communications include:
- Employment inquiries
- Professional networking
- Questions about portfolio content

## Not Accepted
I do not respond to:
- Unsolicited marketing or sales offers
- Requests for free consulting or troubleshooting
- Requests to access third‑party systems
- Security vulnerability submissions containing sensitive data

## Response Time
I attempt to respond within a reasonable timeframe, but responses are not guaranteed and may be delayed.

## Sensitive Information
Do not include passwords, private keys, internal company data, or confidential materials in messages. Any such information will be deleted without review.
